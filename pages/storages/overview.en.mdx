# Storages [#storages]

### Overview [#overview]

Storages are spaces to store media files such as images and videos that are crawled and archived by CabinetJS.

### Available Storages [#available-storages]

Currently, CabinetJS supports the following storages.

1. [Local Storage](/storages/local)
2. [S3 Storage](/storages/s3)
